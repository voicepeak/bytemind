# write-rfc

## Required Sections

1. Context and Problem
2. Goals and Non-Goals
3. Candidate Options
4. Trade-offs
5. Proposed Design
6. Rollout Plan
7. Verification Plan
8. Risks and Mitigations

## Rules

- Mark assumptions explicitly.
- Keep risks testable and concrete.
- Ensure conclusions map to executable next steps.
